Building a More Robust, Reliable, and Helpful Model

More Accurate Answers

GPT-5 is significantly less likely to hallucinate than our previous models. With web search enabled, GPT-5's responses are ~45% less likely to contain factual errors than GPT-4o.

More Honest Responses

GPT-5 more honestly communicates its actions and capabilities to users, especially for tasks which are impossible, underspecified, or missing key tools.

Safer, More Helpful Responses

GPT-5 advances the frontier on safety with new safe completions training, enabling better navigation of dual-use questions and stronger robustness.
